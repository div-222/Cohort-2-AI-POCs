## 5. HR Portal Setup

The HR Portal (`portal.company.com`) is your single window for all HR transactions.

### First Login
```
1. Go to portal.company.com
2. Click "First Time Login"
3. Enter your Employee ID (provided in welcome email)
4. Enter your registered mobile number for OTP verification
5. Set a new password (min 8 characters, 1 uppercase, 1 number, 1 special char)
6. Complete your profile — add a photo, emergency contact, and bank details
```

### Key HR Portal Modules

| Module | What You Can Do |
|--------|----------------|
| My Leave | Apply, view, cancel leave; check leave balance |
| My Payslips | Download monthly payslips |
| My Benefits | View insurance details, submit fitness claims |
| Tax & Investment | Submit IT declarations, view Form 16 |
| Travel | Apply for travel requests, submit expense reports |
| Performance | Set goals, view reviews |
| Policies | Access all HR policies |

## 6. 30-60-90 Day Onboarding Plan

### First 30 Days — Understand & Explore

- Complete all mandatory training modules (list in Section 7).
- Shadow team members to understand workflows.
- Set up all accounts and tools.
- Schedule 1:1 meeting with your manager by end of Week 1.
- Complete HR policy acknowledgment form.
- Attend team standup and sprint ceremonies.

### 31–60 Days — Engage & Contribute

- Begin contributing to assigned tasks with guidance.
- Complete remaining training modules.
- Identify gaps and discuss with manager.
- Participate in team retrospectives.
- Complete 45-day check-in with HR.

### 61–90 Days — Perform & Deliver

- Take ownership of assigned responsibilities.
- Complete the 90-day self-assessment form.
- Have a formal review with your manager.
- Set quarterly OKRs / goals.

## 10. FAQs for New Joiners

**Q: When will I receive my laptop?**  
A: Your laptop will be ready on Day 1. Collect it from the IT Help Desk on Floor 2 after your initial HR meeting.

**Q: When is my first salary credited?**  
A: If you join before the 15th of the month, you will receive a prorated salary in the same month. Joining after the 15th may result in the first salary being credited in the following month.

**Q: How do I apply for leave?**  
A: Log in to the HR Portal → My Leave → Apply for Leave. Please refer to the Leave Policy for entitlement details.

**Q: How do I access company resources from home?**  
A: Install and configure the VPN client (Cisco AnyConnect) on your laptop. Refer to the VPN Setup Guide in the IT Knowledge Base or raise a helpdesk ticket for assistance.

**Q: Who is my HR buddy?**  
A: Your HR buddy will be introduced to you on Day 1. They are your go-to person for any HR-related questions during your first 90 days.
