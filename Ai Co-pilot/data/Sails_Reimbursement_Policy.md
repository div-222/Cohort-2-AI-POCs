## 1. Purpose

This policy defines how Sails Software Solutions reimburses employees for legitimate expenses incurred **on behalf of the Company**, including general business expenses and approved Learning & Development (L&D) investments. It sets out what is eligible, what documentation is required, how requests are approved, and how quickly reimbursements are processed, so that employees are made whole promptly while the Company maintains financial discipline and statutory compliance (including GST and TDS).

---

## 2. Scope and Applicability

This policy applies to all employees who incur authorised expenses on behalf of the Company. L&D reimbursement (Sections 9–10) applies only to **full-time employees who have completed probation** and who meet the L&D eligibility criteria. Where an expense category is governed by a more specific policy (for example, the Leave Travel Allowance under the Travel Policy), that policy governs the tax treatment while this policy governs the reimbursement mechanics.

---

## 3. Definitions and Glossary

- **Reimbursement:** Repayment to an employee for money spent on the Company's behalf.
- **Business Expense:** A cost incurred wholly and necessarily for Company/operational purposes.
- **Original Bill / Receipt:** The primary tax invoice or receipt evidencing the expense.
- **GST Bill:** A tax invoice carrying the Company GST number, required for purchases above the GST threshold.
- **L&D:** Learning & Development — training, certifications, courses, conferences.
- **Clawback / Repayment Agreement:** The obligation to repay reimbursed L&D amounts on early exit.
- **POI:** Proof of Investment (tax-related documentation).
- **Pay Cycle:** The monthly payroll run; payroll is on or before the 1st of the month.
- **HRIS:** greytHR portal at https://sailssoftware.greythr.com/.
- **Company GST Number:** **37AAVCS6208A1Z8**.

## 4. General Principles of Reimbursement

- The Company reimburses expenses **incurred on behalf of the Company** only.
- **Original bills/receipts are mandatory** for all reimbursement claims.
- Expenses must be **reasonable, necessary, and authorised**.
- Reimbursement is **not** salary or income; it is repayment of a Company cost and is not subject to income tax when properly documented.
- Claims without valid supporting documentation **will not be processed**.
- Employees should obtain prior approval for any non-routine or high-value spend.

## 6. Documentation and GST Requirements

- **Original bills/receipts** must be provided for every claim.
- For any purchase **above ₹2,000**, a **GST bill must be obtained quoting the Company GST number — 37AAVCS6208A1Z8**.
- For L&D claims, **proof of payment** and the **completion certificate** are both required.
- For travel claims, retain **tickets and supporting invoices** (see Travel Policy for LTA proof requirements).
- Soft copies should be legible; the Finance Department may request the physical original for verification.

---

## 7. Submission Process

1. Incur the authorised expense and **retain the original bill/receipt** (with GST invoice if > ₹2,000).
2. Upload the claim and supporting documents via the **reimbursement form**:
   `https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=tA8c9P2Fk0mzIHf6-djQf7mRIoZ_gFlAj6Z5wNBW7i5UQzZZWUo3Rk5NTTVQQTNIT0kxUEw2TTcxWi4u`
3. Ensure the correct approver(s) are looped in per the authority matrix (Section 8).
4. Finance verifies the claim against documentation and policy.
5. Approved reimbursements are processed, typically **within one pay cycle**.

## 9. Learning & Development (L&D) Reimbursement

### 9.1 What Qualifies

- Workshops, webinars, and seminars.
- Online courses and professional certification programs.
- Conferences and industry-specific events.
- In-house training and cross-training opportunities.

### 9.2 Pre-Approval

All L&D activities requiring financial investment must be **pre-approved** per the matrix in Section 8.2 before enrolment/payment.

### 9.3 Reimbursement Process

Upon successful completion of the approved L&D activity:
1. Submit **proof of payment** and the **completion certificate**.
2. The L&D committee processes reimbursement per Company financial policies, **typically within one pay cycle**.

### 9.4 Eligibility Criteria

- Minimum **1 year** completed at Sails.
- Latest performance review **above the "meeting expectations"** category.
- **No pending inquiry or disciplinary procedure** as of the request date.
- Eligible for **4 occasions per calendar year**.
- Gap between certifications **must be at least 3 months**.
- Any exceptions require **CEO approval**.

### 9.5 Performance Linkage

Participation in and completion of L&D is factored into performance reviews as evidence of **Learning Agility**, and may support career progression and special-project consideration.

## 10. L&D Repayment Agreement (Clawback)

- If an employee **leaves the Company within one year** of receiving L&D reimbursement for programs/training/certifications priced **above INR 20,000**, they **may be required to repay** the reimbursed amount on a **pro-rata basis**.
- The pro-rata repayment is typically calculated on the unserved portion of the 12-month period from the reimbursement date.
- Repayment may be **deducted from the full-and-final settlement**.

---

## 11. Travel-Related Reimbursement

- Genuine business-travel expenses incurred on the Company's behalf are reimbursable with original supporting documents.
- **Leave Travel Allowance (LTA)** is a **tax-exemption** benefit (not a cash reimbursement) governed by the **Travel Policy**; it requires travel tickets, leave records, and family proof, submitted with POI to Finance **within the same financial year**.
- Travel undertaken as part of a corporate group or on official business **does not qualify** for LTA exemption.

> See the Travel Policy document (SAILS-HR-TRAVEL-2026) for full LTA rules, block periods, and documentation.

## 14. Worked Examples

### 14.1 Sub-₹2,000 Purchase
An employee buys an authorised ₹1,800 accessory. Original receipt is sufficient; a GST bill is not strictly required by the ₹2,000 threshold, but a proper invoice is still recommended.

### 14.2 Above-₹2,000 Purchase
An employee buys an authorised ₹6,500 item. A **GST bill quoting 37AAVCS6208A1Z8** is **mandatory**; without it, the claim will not be processed.

### 14.3 L&D ₹4,000 Certification
Requires **L&D + Manager** approval (Finance and CEO/CTO not required). On completion, submit proof of payment + certificate; reimbursed within one pay cycle.

### 14.4 L&D ₹25,000 Course
Requires **L&D + Manager + Finance + CEO/CTO** approval. Falls under the **clawback** rule: leaving within 12 months may trigger pro-rata repayment.

### 14.5 Clawback Calculation
An employee reimbursed ₹24,000 for a course leaves after **6 months**. Pro-rata repayable on the unserved 6 months ≈ **₹12,000**, recoverable via F&F.

### 14.6 L&D Frequency Limit
An employee requests a 5th certification in one calendar year. This **exceeds the 4-occasions limit** and requires a **CEO exception**.

## 15. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. How do I submit a reimbursement?**
A1. Upload your claim and original receipts via the **reimbursement forms link**; ensure the right approvers are looped in.

**Q2. When do I need a GST bill?**
A2. For any purchase **above ₹2,000**, quoting the Company GST number **37AAVCS6208A1Z8**.

**Q3. How long does reimbursement take?**
A3. Typically **within one pay cycle**.

**Q4. Who approves my L&D request?**
A4. It depends on the amount — manager always; L&D from ₹1,501; Finance from ₹5,001; CEO/CTO from ₹20,001. L&D is always notified.

**Q5. Do I get money back before completing the course?**
A5. No. L&D reimbursement requires **proof of payment and a completion certificate** first.

**Q6. Am I eligible for L&D reimbursement in my first year?**
A6. No. You need **minimum 1 year** at Sails, plus a review **above "meeting expectations"** and no pending disciplinary action.

**Q7. How many L&D claims can I make per year?**
A7. **4 occasions per calendar year**, with **at least 3 months** between certifications.

**Q8. Do I have to repay L&D money if I quit?**
A8. For courses **above ₹20,000**, leaving **within a year** may require **pro-rata repayment**, deductible from F&F.

**Q9. Is LTA a reimbursement?**
A9. No — LTA is a **tax exemption** on actual travel, governed by the Travel Policy, not a cash reimbursement.

**Q10. Will personal purchases be reimbursed?**
A10. No. Only expenses **incurred on behalf of the Company** with valid documentation.

## 16. Roles and Responsibilities

- **Employee:** Obtain approval, retain original/GST receipts, submit via the form, comply with L&D eligibility.
- **Reporting Manager:** Verify business necessity and approve (always required).
- **L&D Committee:** Assess strategic impact, approve L&D, process L&D reimbursements, track eligibility/frequency.
- **Finance:** Validate documentation, ensure GST/TDS compliance, process payments, manage clawbacks.
- **Senior Management / CEO-CTO:** Approve high-value and exception cases.

---

## 17. Compliance and Misuse

- Claims must be honest and fully documented. **Falsifying receipts or claims** is a serious offence and may lead to disciplinary action up to termination.
- Purchases routed around the GST requirement, or unauthorised spend, will not be reimbursed.
- Repeated non-compliance may be escalated to HR/management.
