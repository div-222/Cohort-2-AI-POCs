# HR Travel Policy
**Document Type:** HR Policy  
**Category:** Business Travel & Expenses  
**Version:** 2.0  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Owner:** HR & Finance Department  

---

## 1. Overview

This policy provides guidelines for domestic and international business travel undertaken by employees on behalf of the organization. It defines entitlements, approval processes, and reimbursement guidelines to ensure cost-effective and compliant travel.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time employees and contractors who travel for official business purposes including:
- Client meetings
- Conferences and training programs
- Inter-office transfers
- Field work and site visits

---

## 3. Travel Approval Process

### Pre-Travel Authorization

```
1. Employee raises a Travel Request in the HR Portal
   (portal.company.com → Travel → New Travel Request)
2. Provide: Destination, Purpose, Dates, Estimated Cost
3. Direct Manager approves the travel request
4. For international travel: Additional approval from Department Head + HR
5. Travel team books tickets and accommodation (or employee books and claims)
6. Travel Request ID generated — use this for all expense claims
```

> **Important:** Travel undertaken without prior approval will not be reimbursed.

## 4. Domestic Travel Entitlements

### 4.1 Air Travel

| Grade | Class of Travel |
|-------|----------------|
| Junior (L1–L4) | Economy |
| Senior (L5–L7) | Economy (Business permitted if flight > 3 hrs) |
| Manager & Above (L8+) | Business Class |
| VP and above | Business Class |

**Rules:**
- Book flights at least **5 business days** in advance to avail best fares.
- Flights booked within 48 hours of travel require senior management approval.
- Personal frequent-flyer miles may be accrued but cannot be used to upgrade at company cost.

| City Category | Daily Allowance |

## 6. Travel Expense Claim Process

### Post-Travel Expense Report

```
1. Travel ends → Employee has 10 working days to submit expenses
2. Log in to HR Portal → Travel → Submit Expense Report
3. Select your Travel Request ID
4. Enter itemized expenses with dates and amounts
5. Upload all receipts (flights, hotel, cab, meals if above per diem)
6. Submit → Manager approves within 3 days
7. Finance processes within 7 working days
8. Amount credited to employee's salary account
```

## 7. Additional Guidelines

### Cancellation & Changes
- Cancellations due to company requirements: Full refund credited.
- Cancellations due to employee reasons: Employee bears cancellation charges.
- Date changes: Must be approved by the manager before incurring change fees.

### Upgrades
- Employees may pay out-of-pocket for upgrades beyond their entitled class.
- Company will reimburse only the entitled class amount.

### Accompanied Travel
- Family members are not covered under this policy.
- Spouse / family travel expenses will not be reimbursed.

### Travel Insurance
- All international travelers are automatically covered under company travel insurance.
- Employees must carry their travel insurance card while traveling abroad.
- For medical emergencies abroad, contact the insurance helpline: +91-1800-XXX-XXXX.

## 8. FAQs

**Q: Who approves my travel request?**  
A: Your direct manager approves domestic travel. International travel additionally requires Department Head and HR approval.

**Q: What is the per diem rate for travel to Mumbai?**  
A: ₹1,500 per day (Metro city rate).

**Q: How long do I have to submit my travel expense report?**  
A: Within 10 working days of returning from travel.

**Q: Can I book business class for a 2-hour domestic flight if I'm a Manager?**  
A: Yes, Managers (L8+) are entitled to Business Class for all domestic flights.

**Q: What if my hotel bill exceeds the entitled limit?**  
A: Only the entitled limit will be reimbursed. The excess amount is to be borne by the employee.
