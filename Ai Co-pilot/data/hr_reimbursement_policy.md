# HR Reimbursement Policy
**Document Type:** HR Policy  
**Category:** Expense & Reimbursement  
**Version:** 1.4  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Owner:** HR & Finance Department  

---

## 1. Overview

This policy governs the reimbursement of expenses incurred by employees in the course of their official duties. All reimbursement claims must be legitimate, supported by original receipts, and submitted within the stipulated timeframe.

---

## 2. Eligible Expense Categories

### 2.1 Mobile / Phone Allowance

| Grade | Monthly Limit |
|-------|--------------|
| Junior (L1–L3) | ₹500 / month |
| Mid-Level (L4–L6) | ₹1,000 / month |
| Senior (L7–L9) | ₹1,500 / month |
| Manager & Above | ₹2,000 / month |

- Employees must submit a copy of their mobile bill.
- Personal calls are not covered.

---

### 2.2 Internet Allowance (Work from Home)

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Monthly Limit | ₹1,000 / month |
| Eligibility | Employees working from home ≥ 15 days/month |
| Documentation | Internet service provider bill |

---

### 2.3 Meal / Food Reimbursement

| Scenario | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Working late (beyond 8 PM) | ₹300 per incident |
| Official meetings (client/vendor) | Actual cost, up to ₹1,500 per head |
| Team events (with manager approval) | Actual cost, up to ₹800 per head |

**Rules:**
- Alcohol expenses are **not reimbursable**.
- Receipts must include date, vendor name, and itemized bill.
- Manager must pre-approve team meal expenses above ₹5,000.

---

### 2.4 Local Conveyance

| Mode | Reimbursement Rate |
|------|--------------------|
| Auto-rickshaw | Actuals (with receipt) |
| Cab (Ola / Uber) | Actuals (app receipt) |
| Personal Vehicle (2-wheeler) | ₹4 per km |
| Personal Vehicle (4-wheeler) | ₹10 per km |

**Rules:**
- Local conveyance must be for official work purposes only.
- Claims must be supported by cab receipts, trip screenshots, or mileage logs.
- Maximum reimbursement without prior approval: ₹1,000 per day.

---

### 2.5 Training & Certification

| Item | Coverage |
|------|---------|
| External certification exam fee | 100% (up to ₹25,000/year) |
| Online course subscription | 100% (up to ₹15,000/year) |
| Training materials / books | 75% (up to ₹5,000/year) |

**Conditions:**
- Training must be relevant to the employee's current or upcoming role.
- Pre-approval from the department manager and HR is mandatory.
- If an employee leaves within 1 year of certification reimbursement, a recovery clause applies (50% recovery).

---

### 2.6 Medical Reimbursement

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Annual Limit | ₹15,000 per employee |
| Family Coverage | Spouse + 2 dependent children |
| Eligible Expenses | Doctor consultation, medicines, diagnostic tests |
| Documentation | Original bills and prescriptions |

- Cosmetic procedures, dental (non-emergency), and vision correction surgery are excluded.
- Group Medical Insurance is separate from medical reimbursement (see Insurance Policy).

---

## 3. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses will **not** be reimbursed under any circumstances:

- Personal purchases unrelated to work
- Alcohol and tobacco
- Traffic fines and penalties
- Membership fees for personal clubs or gyms
- Expenses incurred by family members (unless explicitly covered)
- Expenses without supporting receipts
- Expenses claimed after 60 days of incurrence

---

## 4. Reimbursement Claim Process

### Step-by-Step Process

```
1. Collect all original receipts / bills
2. Log in to the HR Portal → Finance → Submit Reimbursement
3. Select expense category
4. Enter amount, date, and purpose
5. Upload scanned copies of receipts
6. Submit for manager approval
7. Manager approves within 3 working days
8. Finance team reviews and processes within 7 working days
9. Amount credited to employee's registered bank account
```

### Claim Deadlines

| Period | Last Date to Submit |
|--------|-------------------|
| January – March | April 15 |
| April – June | July 15 |
| July – September | October 15 |
| October – December | January 10 (following year) |

> Claims submitted after the quarterly deadline may not be processed.

## 7. FAQs

**Q: How do I apply for reimbursement?**  
A: Log in to the HR Portal at `portal.company.com`, navigate to Finance → Submit Reimbursement, fill in the details, attach receipts, and submit for manager approval.

**Q: What is the deadline to submit a reimbursement claim?**  
A: Claims must be submitted within 60 days of incurring the expense, and within the quarterly deadline dates listed in Section 4.

**Q: What happens if I lose my receipt?**  
A: Claims without original receipts are generally not processed. For small amounts (under ₹200), a self-declaration form may be accepted with manager approval.

**Q: Is my gym membership reimbursable?**  
A: No. Personal gym memberships are not reimbursable under this policy.

**Q: Can I claim reimbursement for a certification I took on my own?**  
A: Yes, if it is relevant to your role and pre-approved by your manager and HR. Retrospective approvals may not be accepted.
