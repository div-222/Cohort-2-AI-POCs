## 1. Overview

This document outlines the insurance coverage and employee benefits available to all eligible employees and their dependents. These benefits are part of the organization's commitment to employee well-being and financial security.

---

## 2. Group Medical Insurance (GMI)

### 2.1 Coverage Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Sum Insured | ₹5,00,000 per family per year |
| Premium | Fully paid by the company |
| Coverage | Employee + Spouse + 2 dependent children (up to age 25) |
| Dependent Parents | Optional top-up at subsidized employee cost |
| Policy Type | Floater (shared among family members) |
| Pre-existing Conditions | Covered from Day 1 |
| Maternity Cover | Included (normal delivery: ₹50,000; C-section: ₹75,000) |
| New Born Baby | Covered from Day 1 of birth |

### 2.2 What is Covered

- In-patient hospitalization (minimum 24 hours)
- Pre-hospitalization expenses (30 days before admission)
- Post-hospitalization expenses (60 days after discharge)
- Day-care procedures (surgeries not requiring 24-hr hospitalization)
- Ambulance charges (up to ₹2,500 per incident)
- ICU / ICCU charges
- Doctor's fees, diagnostic tests, medicines

### 2.3 What is NOT Covered

- Cosmetic / aesthetic treatments
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Fertility treatments / IVF
- Non-allopathic treatments (unless AYUSH – see below)
- Dental treatment (unless arising from an accident)
- Vision correction surgery (LASIK)
- Alcohol / drug-related conditions

### 2.4 AYUSH Coverage

Ayurvedic, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy (AYUSH) treatments at government-registered hospitals are covered up to **₹25,000 per year**.

## 3. Group Personal Accident Insurance (GPA)

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Sum Insured | 48x monthly CTC (max ₹1 Crore) |
| Coverage | Accidental death, permanent disability, partial disability |
| Premium | Fully paid by the company |
| Coverage Period | 24x7, worldwide |

### Benefit Table

| Incident | Benefit Payout |
|---------|----------------|
| Accidental Death | 100% of Sum Insured |
| Permanent Total Disability | 100% of Sum Insured |
| Loss of two limbs / eyes | 100% of Sum Insured |
| Loss of one limb / eye | 50% of Sum Insured |
| Permanent Partial Disability | As per disability scale |
| Temporary Total Disability | 1% of SI per week (max 104 weeks) |

## 4. Group Term Life Insurance (GTL)

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Sum Insured | 36x monthly CTC (min ₹10,00,000) |
| Premium | Fully paid by the company |
| Nominee | Employee-designated nominee |
| Coverage | Death due to any cause (including illness) |

- Nominee can be updated once per year during the open enrollment window.

## 5. Provident Fund (PF)

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Employee Contribution | 12% of Basic Salary |
| Employer Contribution | 12% of Basic Salary |
| Interest Rate | As declared by EPFO (currently ~8.15% p.a.) |
| Withdrawal | On resignation / retirement (with applicable rules) |
| UAN | Unique Account Number allotted at joining |

**How to access PF details:**
- Visit `epfindia.gov.in` with your UAN and password.
- PF statements are also available in the HR Portal.

## 6. Gratuity

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Eligibility | Minimum 5 years of continuous service |
| Calculation | (15 × Last Monthly Salary × Number of Years) / 26 |
| Maximum Limit | ₹20,00,000 |
| Payment | At the time of resignation, retirement, or death/disability |

## 9. Insurance Enrollment

- All insurance benefits are **auto-enrolled** at the time of joining.
- Employees must submit dependent details and nominee information within **30 days** of joining via: `HR Portal → My Benefits → Insurance Enrollment`.
- Changes to dependents can only be made during the **Annual Open Enrollment** (January of each year) or within **30 days of a qualifying life event** (marriage, childbirth, etc.).

## 10. FAQs

**Q: Does the group medical insurance cover my parents?**  
A: Yes, parents can be added at a subsidized premium paid by the employee. The cost is deducted from the monthly salary. Contact HR to enroll parents.

**Q: What is the sum insured under group medical insurance?**  
A: ₹5,00,000 per family per year under a floater policy.

**Q: How do I file a medical insurance claim?**  
A: For cashless treatment, visit an empanelled network hospital and show your insurance card. For reimbursement claims, submit bills to HR within 30 days of discharge.

**Q: When am I eligible for gratuity?**  
A: After completing a minimum of 5 years of continuous service with the company.

**Q: How do I update my PF nominee?**  
A: Log in to the EPFO portal with your UAN at `epfindia.gov.in` and update the nominee details.
