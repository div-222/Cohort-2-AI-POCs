## 1. Purpose

This guide consolidates the full range of benefits available to employees of Sails Software Solutions — financial, statutory, developmental, and lifestyle — in one place. It points to the detailed policy documents where applicable and gives employees a single reference for understanding what they are entitled to, when, and how to access it.

---

## 2. Scope and Applicability

Benefits apply to employees per their category and tenure. Some benefits (the optional holiday, L&D reimbursement) require completion of probation or minimum service; these conditions are noted in each section and summarised in Section 18.

---

## 3. Definitions and Glossary

- **CTC / Salary Structure:** The composition of pay, including basic, allowances, and benefit components such as LTA and PF.
- **Earned Leave (EL):** 16 days/year paid leave (1.33/month).
- **Comp-Off:** Paid day off for an approved, client-billed extra workday.
- **Sum Insured:** Health cover amount (₹3,00,000 base).
- **Gratuity:** Long-service lump sum (5-year eligibility).
- **L&D:** Learning & Development benefit.
- **HRIS:** greytHR at https://sailssoftware.greythr.com/.
- **Curry Plan:** Complimentary lunchtime curries for employees in PGs/hostels without family.

## 5. Compensation and Salary

- Payroll runs **monthly**; salary is paid **on or before the 1st** of the month for the previous month's work.
- **Salary slips** are downloadable from the **HRIS** (https://sailssoftware.greythr.com/login.do) once payroll is released.
- Salary is deposited into the **ICICI salary account**.
- Employees with PF deduction must provide their **UAN/PF number**.
- **TDS** is deducted per prevailing IT rules; employees may file an **IT declaration** in the HRIS to optimise tax (changes before the **10th** of a month apply to the next cycle). **POI** is due in **October** or at exit.
- **Form 16** is shared after the financial year ends.
- **Compensation is confidential.** Discussing salary with colleagues is **strongly discouraged and could lead to termination**.

---

## 6. Leave Benefits

- **16 working days** of paid earned leave per year (**1.33/month**).
- Probationers accrue monthly; new joiners receive pro-rata.
- No carry-forward; encashment only at management discretion.
- Full details in the **Leave Policy** (SAILS-HR-LEAVE-2026).

---

## 7. Holidays

- **11 holidays/year**: **10 common (mandatory)** + **1 optional** (post-probation only, 2 weeks' notice).
- The 2026 holiday calendar is published at the start of the year (see Leave Policy §9).

---

## 8. Health Insurance

- **Free ₹3,00,000** group cover for **employee + spouse + 2 children**.
- **Top-up** available **at own cost**.
- Coverage is valid **only during employment** and **ceases on separation**.
- Full details in the **Insurance Policy** (SAILS-HR-INSURANCE-2026).

---

## 9. Gratuity

- **Employer-funded**, per the **Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972**.
- Eligible after **5 years of continuous service**.
- Formula: `(15 × last drawn basic × completed years) / 26`.

---

## 10. Provident Fund

- Statutory retirement savings for employees with PF in their structure.
- Provide **UAN/PF number** for timely credit.

---

## 11. Leave Travel Allowance (LTA)

- **Tax exemption** on **domestic** travel taken **on leave**.
- **Twice per four-year block** (current block **2023–2026**).
- Requires tickets, leave records, and family proof; submit to Finance within the FY.
- Full details in the **Travel Policy** (SAILS-HR-TRAVEL-2026).

---

## 12. Learning & Development (L&D)

- Reimbursed workshops, courses, certifications, and conferences.
- Eligibility: **min 1 year**, review **above "meeting expectations"**, no pending disciplinary action; **4 occasions/year**, **≥3-month gap**.
- Approval matrix and **clawback** (>₹20,000, exit within 1 year) in the **Reimbursement Policy** (SAILS-FIN-REIMB-2026).
- L&D engagement contributes to **Learning Agility** in performance reviews.

---

## 13. Compensatory Off

- A **paid day off** for an **approved, client-billed** extra day worked.
- Email HR (manager in CC) with client approval + billing proof, **within the same month**.
- Use **within the calendar year**; no carry-forward; no encashment.

---

## 14. Maternity Benefit

- Up to **6 months**: either **basic pay throughout**, or **full pay for 3 months + unpaid for 3 months**.
- Confirm the chosen option **4 weeks** before starting.
- Full details in the **Leave Policy** §10.

---

## 15. Workplace Flexibility

- Work starts at **10:30 AM** (8 hours, excluding lunch/breaks).
- Employees may **step away for personal work** with manager/project-leader approval.
- Using this flexibility for **more than 4 hours in a month** requires applying for **leave**.
- Abuse may attract severe action; flexibility does not excuse missed deadlines.

---

## 16. Company Equipment

- Each employee receives a **laptop and mouse**, configured for productive work.
- An **external monitor** is provided **based on need and availability**.
- Employees are responsible for the **safe keeping** of equipment and peripherals (lost unattended items are charged to the employee).
- **Mishandling** damage (beyond normal wear and tear) is **deducted from salary** with a bill produced.
- The **IT team** handles network issues and server access (AWS/Azure/GCP) on a need basis.
- A **cloud-connected printer** is available; print sparingly for sustainability.

---

## 17. Pantry, Coffee, and Curry Plan

- **Free coffee** via the vending machine on the **5th floor** (in good times).
- The **pantry** carries snack items, restocked regularly (availability not guaranteed).
- **Curry Plan:** Complimentary lunchtime **curries** for employees residing in **PGs/hostels without family**.

## 20. Worked Examples

### 20.1 New Joiner Benefits
A confirmed-track new joiner gets pro-rata leave immediately, health cover from enrolment, and the optional holiday only after probation.

### 20.2 L&D After One Year
An employee at 13 months with a strong review can claim L&D reimbursement (up to 4 times/year, 3-month gaps).

### 20.3 Curry Plan Eligibility
An employee living in a hostel without family qualifies for complimentary lunchtime curries; an employee living with family does not.

### 20.4 Equipment Damage
An employee cracks a laptop screen by mishandling it; the repair cost (beyond wear and tear) is **deducted from salary**.

### 20.5 Salary Confidentiality
An employee openly compares salaries with a colleague — this is **strongly discouraged and could lead to termination**.

## 21. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. When is salary paid?**
A1. **On or before the 1st** of each month, into your **ICICI salary account**.

**Q2. Where do I get my payslip?**
A2. From the **HRIS (greytHR)** once payroll is released.

**Q3. Can I discuss my salary with colleagues?**
A3. No — it's **strongly discouraged and could lead to termination**.

**Q4. What health cover do I get?**
A4. **₹3,00,000** free for **employee + spouse + 2 children**; top-up at own cost.

**Q5. When can I take the optional holiday?**
A5. **After probation**, with **2 weeks' notice**.

**Q6. Am I eligible for L&D reimbursement?**
A6. After **1 year** with a review above "meeting expectations" and no pending disciplinary action.

**Q7. Do I get a monitor?**
A7. **Based on need and availability**, in addition to a laptop and mouse.

**Q8. What's the curry plan?**
A8. Free lunchtime **curries** for employees in **PGs/hostels without family**.

**Q9. When am I eligible for gratuity?**
A9. After **5 years** of continuous service.

**Q10. Is there free coffee?**
A10. Yes — via the **5th-floor vending machine** (in good times).
